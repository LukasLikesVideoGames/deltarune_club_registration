Description:

Welcome to the Deltarune Club, a community for fans of DELTARUNE, the hit RPG by Toby Fox, creator of Undertale. Whether you're a longtime fan or just discovering the game, this club is your gateway into the strange, funny, and heartfelt world of Kris, Susie, Ralsei, and all the unforgettable characters of the Dark World. Determined? In a trance? Full of eggs? You’ll fit right in.

What to look forward to:

  • Discussing and analyzing chapters 1-4
  • Exploring game mechanics, secrets, and lore
  • Sharing fan art, music, theories, and cosplay
  • Hosting playthrough sessions, voice readings, and theory nights
  • Debating the BIG (SHOT) questions with each other
